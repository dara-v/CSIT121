Mrs. Carter's Cake

Wyoming Carter

  • Sugar
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Lemon Extract
  • Baking Powder
  1. Add 2 cups of sugar
  2. Add 2 sticks of unsalted butter
  3. Cream sugar and butter
  4. Add 6 eggs
  5. Add 3 cups of flour
  6. Add 1 cup of milk
  7. Add 1 tsp. of vanilla extract
  8. Add 1 tsp. of lemon extract
  9. Add 3 tsp. of baking powder
  10. Preheat oven to 350Â°
  11. Cook for 1 hour or until golden brown

This recipe was originally made by my great grandmother, Wyoming Carter. This recipe has been passed down to my grandmother. She has made this cake on special occasions. I enjoy helping my grandmother bake this cake because it is a fun activity.
